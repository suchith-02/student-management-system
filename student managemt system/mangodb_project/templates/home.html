About the College

PESITM College of Engineering, Shivamogga is a premier institution known for its dedication to innovation, research, and student success. We foster a vibrant academic environment and shape students into future-ready professionals.

About the Courses

Computer Science

Focuses on software engineering, data structures, databases, and operating systems. Prepares students for careers in development and research.

Information Technology

Covers networking, cybersecurity, and system administration. Emphasizes practical implementation and infrastructure management.

Artificial Intelligence

Introduces students to machine learning, neural networks, deep learning, and real-time AI applications with project-based learning.

Information Science

Focuses on information theory, analytics, software development, and system design using modern technologies.

Electrical Science and Engineering

Covers power systems, electronics, embedded systems, and electrical machines. Offers both core theory and lab experience.
